Description

Prothom Alo is the largest Bangla newspaper from Dhaka (Bangladesh). This is the official app of Prothom Alo Bangla to provide its readers with news in Bangla on the go.

It has been designed with a fresh look and feel to bring you more content, greater personalization and better overall performance. So you can get more of the news that matters to you.

Features of this app:
* All the latest news, features, most-read news from Prothom Alo
* Latest & Most Read News. Keep on top of popular stories across Prothom Alo, regularly updated throughout the day
* Video gallery
* Push notifications for breaking news
* Photo galleries and full-screen images
* Comment on a particular news
* Share news on different social media channels

Install the app now and get the experience of the most trusted Bangla newspaper on your mobile device.

Prothom Alo is the most popular newspaper from Bangladesh and is published from Dhaka (Bangladesh).
